Clubs Fore Kids is non-profit organization on the Central Coast that offers free after school golf programs for students ages six to 18 from underprivileged school districts.

Our mission is to impact the lives of youth by fostering an environment where honesty, wellbeing, compassion, and empowerment are promoted values through the game of golf for underprivileged youth.

Sessions can be accessed year-round for the Winter, Spring, Fall, and Summer programs.
